User Research

Usability Study & Findings

I want to make sure that newly graduates get enough attention before entering university so that they don’t face difficulties in choosing the right field. I conducted interviews and created empathy maps to understand the users I’m designing for and their needs. A primary user group identified through research was those who are confused about their life goals and don’t know where to focus. This user group highlighted their pain points regarding the fact. But they’re not the only one. Another user group wants someone to mentor them so they don’t fail in the long run.
